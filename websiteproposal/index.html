Raftrush Website Proposal

Objective

I propose the development of a website for "Raftrush", a new outdoor adventure company that offers rafting tours, camping trips, and hiking expeditions.
The website will serve as a platform to promote and sell the company's services, attract new customers,
and provide information about the company's values, mission, and team.

Scope of Work

The scope of work for this project includes:

  • Designing and developing a responsive website using HTML and CSS/li>

  • Implementing a clean and modern design that aligns with the company's brand and values
  • Integrating high-quality images and videos to showcase the company's tours and destinations
  • Ensuring cross-browser compatibility and responsiveness on different devices
  • Optimizing the website for search engines (SEO) to improve visibility and ranking
  • Integrating a booking and payment system to allow customers to reserve and pay for tours online
